Our Story

Founded in 2020, "For the People" began as a small community initiative tackling food insecurity in urban Johannesburg. What started as a handful of volunteers planting vegetable gardens in vacant lots has grown into a registered NPO dedicated to empowering communities through garden maintenance and agricultural education.

🌿 Our Mission

To cultivate healthy communities by providing knowledge, tools, and support for sustainable gardening — ensuring everyone has access to fresh, nutritious food.

✨ Our Vision

A world where every neighborhood is self-sufficient, green, and nourished by the food it grows.
