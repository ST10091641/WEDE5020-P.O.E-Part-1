Organization overview: For The People was founded in 2020, it began as a small community initiatve in response to rising food insecurity and the decline of green spaces in urban areas. What started as a handful of volunteers planting vegatable gardens in vacant lots has grown into a registered non-profit organization . The Organization is dedicated to empowering communities through hands-on garden maintenance and agricultural education. The mission is to cultivate healthy communities by providing the knowledge, tools, and support needed for sustainable gardening, ensuring that every person has access to fresh, nutritious food.
Website Goals & Objevtives: Provide a comprehensive database on which herbs, vegetables, and fruits are benefical for speceifc health needs.
Increase volunteer sign-ups by 50% within the first year, build a community forum for gardeners to share tips and soil conditions.
Key Performance Indicators: Number of volunteer application form submissions, Page views and time spent on the "Soil & Seed" database, Number of newsletter subscriptions, click through the "Donate" button.
Key Features: Homepage; a hero section with the tagline "Garden Maintenance" For The People.
Soil & Seed Database: An interactive, filterable database where users can select a soil type (clay, Sandy, Loamy, Chalky) to see which seeds grow the best, followed by the health benefits of those plants.
Services: Information on the garden maintenance services offered, Get Involved; Volunteer registration, donation portal, and event calender.
